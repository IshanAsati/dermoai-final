Dermatological Image Analysis

DermoAI performs supervised classification of dermoscopic and clinical skin imagery across a fixed taxonomy of 12 lesion classes. Each submission returns a ranked differential, a calibrated confidence figure, and a severity triage tier. The system is a decision-support instrument — it quantifies visual similarity and does not establish a diagnosis.
